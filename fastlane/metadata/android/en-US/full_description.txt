FlutterPlotter is an open-source marine chart plotter built for sailors who want a simple, touch-first navigation experience on their phone or tablet.

Features:
- OpenStreetMap + OpenSeaMap charts with course-up mode
- Real-time GPS vessel tracking
- NMEA 0183 input over TCP/UDP for heading, depth, wind, and speed
- AIS target display with CPA/TCPA collision alarms
- Route and waypoint management with GPX import/export
- Instrument panel showing SOG, COG, heading, depth, wind, and cross-track error
- Offline chart tile downloads for sailing without internet
- Night mode with red-tinted display to preserve dark adaptation

Connect to your boat's NMEA multiplexer or Wi-Fi gateway to receive live instrument data, or use the built-in GPS for basic position tracking.
